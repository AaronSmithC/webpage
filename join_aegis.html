Join the Mission: Aegis Station

The Vision

Aegis Station is a bold step toward humanity’s future in orbit: a three-ring, self-sustaining world designed for long-term habitation, exploration, and innovation. More than a space station, it’s a city in the stars—a place to live, thrive, and build the next chapter of civilization.

The Opportunity

This is your invitation to help shape a historic project at the ground level. Aegis Station is in active development, and I’m building a team of trusted collaborators to assist with engineering, design, outreach, strategy, and more. Your contributions will help bring this vision to life—on the page, online, and eventually in orbit.

What I’m Offering

  • Creative Contribution: Influence systems, visuals, or messaging within a well-defined framework.
  • Public Recognition: Your name, title, and role acknowledged on the official site and in project materials.
  • Portfolio Credit: Use your contributions for your own professional visibility.
  • Advisory Opportunities: High-impact contributors may be invited to an advisory circle for future phases.
  • Right of First Consideration: When funding becomes available, you’ll be first in line for contract roles or compensation.

What This Is Not (Yet)

  • This is not an offer of equity, shared ownership, or decision-making control.
  • This is not a salaried position or long-term employment (though that may change in the future).
  • This is not a startup co-founder arrangement—I remain the principal architect and creative lead.

Why Join?

  • To help define the first great orbital civilization.
  • To leave your mark on a project with the potential to shape the 21st century.
  • To collaborate with others who believe that the future is something we build.

Let’s build the future in orbit.
