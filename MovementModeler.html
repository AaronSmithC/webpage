Pipeline-ready metadata, built in NEW

Tag your motion captures with structured metadata before you export. No more separate labeling steps — your JSON arrives downstream ready to sort, filter, and process.

  • Activity label — Pick from a built-in vocabulary: Gait, Sit → Stand, Balance, Dance, Upper Limb, Stairs, and more. Or type a custom label.
  • Camera angle — Tag as frontal, sagittal, or other so downstream scripts can handle perspective-specific analysis automatically.
  • Subject selection — In multi-person scenes, mark which tracked person is the primary subject. The index embeds directly in the export.

  "fps": 15.0,

  },
  "frames": [ ... ]
}
